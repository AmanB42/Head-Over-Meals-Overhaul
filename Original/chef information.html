Star Chef

Chef Aman has been trained by the most talented individuals in the restaurant industry. He began cooking at the age of 5 and his expertise has only progressed from there. At the age of 13, he enrolled in the most prestigious culinary school. Only 2 years later did the world renound Chef Gordon Ramsey take him under his wing. Chef Aman trained with Ramsey for over 2 years, learning all the secrets to becoming a true master chef. Now with over 20 years under his belt, Chef Aman is a world-renowned chef with 32 Michelin Stars (the highest ever held by a chef), and has helped start over 10 five star restaurants, including Gusteai's in Paris, France.
